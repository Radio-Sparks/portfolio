Description

This is an example of how AR could be implemented into a paper map allowing guests of a museum to know more about an exhibit so they can choose to visit. The museum map is based off of the Denver Museum of Nature and Science second level however we chose to replace some exhibits with others from around the museum. I am not affiliated with the Denver Museum of Nature and Science in any way and this project is completely unofficial.
