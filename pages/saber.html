Description

Something toxic has polluted the minds of many innocent animals, Now it’s up to this one brave chickadee to save everyone from calamity and corruption. Slaughter to save their souls. Fight as a bold, and fearless Black-Capped Chickadee. Story-driven and heart-wrenching, this 3D RPG is inspired by “The Falconeer,” “The Pathless,” and “Going Under."

My Role


Soul Saber was a class project for Game Design 1. I was the lead programmer and programmed both the enemy AI and player movement and controls. Additionally I made some custom shaders for the game such as the black sludge and polluted water that can be seen around the level.
