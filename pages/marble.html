Description

Stunt Marble was a submission to the GDYU Game Jam with the theme: Tether. It's a score chase stunt game inspired by skateboarding games like Tony Hawk's Pro Skater. You control a marble rolling around a skate park inspired course. The only controls are placing a tether that your marble is attached to by a chain and speeding up your marble.
