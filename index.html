Description

Welcome to your contracted position as an on-line therapy help line attendant. Your job is to help our users with therapy related questions through text conversation over the inter-net. And if you casually point them towards Mirage Mental Health Services On-Line Therapy you get a tidy bonus to your contracted rate.

You Are Eliza was part of Otter Jam 2023, with the theme: Virtual Beasts. Submissions to the jam were required to include AI technology in the process of development, or AI as a theme of the game. You Are Eliza technically has both.

You play as a worker for an on-line therapy provider, answering user's questions and attempting to direct them toward's your employer's services. The users you are interacting with are powered by a modified version of ELIZA. One of the first ever chatbot AIs, originally developed in 1966.

Description

Stunt Marble was a submission to the GDYU Game Jam with the theme: Tether. It's a score chase stunt game inspired by skateboarding games like Tony Hawk's Pro Skater. You control a marble rolling around a skate park inspired course. The only controls are placing a tether that your marble is attached to by a chain and speeding up your marble.

Description

Blink’s Odyssey is a short action adventure video game in the style of early 3D video games from the late ‘90s. It features graphics and gameplay reminiscent of the games of that era. The game takes inspiration from games like The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time, and Banjo Kazooie. Blink’s Odyssey stars a cat named Blink who must learn to harness elemental powers to overcome exploration and combat challenges to uncover the secrets of the ruined area he finds himself in.

Description

I am not affiliated with the Denver Museum of Nature and Science in any way and this project is completely unofficial.
